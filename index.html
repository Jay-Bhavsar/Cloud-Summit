Cloud Summit


JECRC Cloud Summit is an event organized by JECRC Foundation to promote and acknowledge the importance of cloud computing.

We're giving up-and-coming talent a place to congregate, exchange thoughts and opinions, interact with like-minded individuals, and talk about their plans with professionals in the field with some cultural activities to kick off this voyage with a bit of wit and mint.

COMMITMENT

Our mission is to empower students from different backgrounds and encourage them about the hottest subjects in the tech business today, gain practical advice on how to execute your ideas and plans, and have an unforgettable experience that will benefit you long beyond the event.


You'll have the chance to learn the most recent tips on how to use the cloud to your advantage from our opening keynote through sessions to spotlights on participants and partners.

Learn about breakthrough solutions in data, machine learning, collaboration, security, sustainability, and other areas first. You'll hear from experts, consider customer viewpoints, participate in interactive demos, and acquire insightful information that will speed up your career change.
